# Support

Need assistance with Copliot Enigma? Choose the right channel for your request.

## Self-service resources

- [README](./Readme.md) for project overview and quick start
- [ROADMAP](./ROADMAP.md) to understand upcoming features
- [GOVERNANCE](./GOVERNANCE.md) for decision-making processes

## Filing issues

Use [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/danycrafts/copliot-enigma/issues) for:

- Bug reports with reproducible steps and logs
- Feature requests backed by user stories
- Documentation gaps or suggestions

Issues are triaged twice per week. Expect an initial response within **3 business days**.

## Security or privacy concerns

Do **not** open a public issue. Follow the instructions in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) and email `security@copliot-enigma.dev`.

## Community chat

Join the `#copliot-enigma` channel in the Copliot Slack workspace (invite via issue template). Conversations here are best-effort and not part of the official support SLA.

## Escalation

If a blocking issue receives no response within 5 business days, mention `@danycrafts` in the issue or email `support@copliot-enigma.dev` with a link to the ticket.
